WOMEN IN TECH

Do you believe, the ratio of women in core tech job is not upto the mark? Do you want to change the scenario?

Women In Tech is the talk of our town! Is it so in your region as well? Why do we even need such a campaign? Let's get to the root of the problem and understand this. Not many women are encouraged to follow their passion for coding due to various socio-economic factors in India. Lets talk about the process we have initiatiated to help improve the situation:

Attend our WIT Events. Everyone attending the meeting has to introduce themselves. Our coordinators would help match aspiring tech women with their mentors. Once the matches are made, aspiring women have an experienced tech women by their sides to support them throughout!
